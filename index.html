Chelsey Gravel Designs

As a designer with a background in teaching children, I enjoy learning about people from people. How people interact with technology and how design plays such a large role in a users experience.

I enjoy working on my own with all of the resources available, or with a team for inspiration, feedback and accountability when joining together to meet goals.
